## Live Avatar-On Smoke

Accepted by user/operator on 2026-06-29.

Smoke checklist:

1. Turn Avatar mode ON.
2. Start talking.
3. Ask for a multi-sentence response, for example:
   "Give me two short sentences about why phishing training matters."
4. Confirm voice input reaches the agent and voice output returns.
5. Confirm the avatar shifts state while listening, thinking, and speaking.
6. Confirm light hand/arm gestures appear while the avatar speaks.
7. Confirm lip-sync stays aligned across both sentences.

Live UAT notes:

- Initial R5 live test worked voice-to-voice, but speaking gaze felt unnatural
  because the avatar sometimes looked around/down while talking.
- Accepted behavior: while speaking, eyes and head stay steady with eye contact;
  ambient looking/scanning is allowed outside speaking.
- Implemented speaking gaze lock with TalkingHead fixed morph targets and
  released those locks outside speaking.
- User accepted this behavior as "fine for now".
